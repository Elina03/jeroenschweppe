About Me

Hi, This is Jeroen Schweppe from Munich, Germany. I'm 18 years old and I love composing for film and media. I started taking piano lessons at the age of 7 and started learning the double bass four years later.

I've already scored a couple of projects and short films for film students and friends and gained initial experience so far. I also won first place in the "Cinesamples" scoring competition. If you are interested in a collaboration, just contact me.
